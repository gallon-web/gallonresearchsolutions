Data Collection

Accuracy in data collection is essential in maintaining the integrity of the research. At Gallon we use preferred methodologies to gather the reliable data which is used to measure the certainty of business. Few of the different methodologies we practice at our workplace are mentioned below:

  • Online invites through emails for the wide array of respondents for surveying the need.
  • CATI – Computer Assisted Telephone Interviewing, most advantageous technique for corporate sales as it permits the interviewer to educate the respondents on importance of timely and accurate data.
  • CAPI – Computer Assisted Personal Interviewing, under the supervision of the interviewer the respondents are interviewed with right set of questions preferable for long questions.
  • Offline/ fieldwork – involves collective discussions, direct observation and face to face interviews.

Distorted results are handled in order to avoid misleading researchers who can be productive in the unbiased avenues of investigation. At Gallon we prevent consequences of improperly collected data by performing the surveys right first time.
